S&S FOREVER
Completely overhauls the Bandit manufacturer into S&S from Borderlands 1.

List of Changes;
All Bandit items (excluding class mods and some unique/legendary/seraph items) have been reskinned and renamed. This includes gemstone skins (now Topaz) and prefixes. Names and prefixes are based off of space objects and the like (List of Prefixes and Titles can be found below. Only "Bandit" thing that remains is the logo in the item cards.
S&S Weapons have been globally rebalanced. The stats should be fairly balanced in all aspects, except for the main feature.
Legendary Weapons, the Tattler, and the Sawbar have been replaced or adjusted. Details down below.
Main feature of S&S weapons is their "limitless" magazine. The entire ammo pool makes up your magazine (meaning you can fire off an entire ammo pool's worth of ammo before needing to reload.)
S&S weapons have a 25% increased chance to inflict a status effect compared to other non-Maliwan manufacturers.
Magazine and Reload accessories would be largely redundant with the magazine effect. Magazine accessories now regenerate ammo slowly (even Launchers), and reload effects increase Elemental Status Effect chance and damage.
Stat penalties from Tinderbox removed.
CHOPPER now only consumes four ammo per shot and does not automatically empty the magazine.

Replacers

Madhous! -> Serpens
Slagga -> Gemini
Sledge's Shotgun -> The Perseids
Gub -> Aries
Badaboom - > Cassiopeia
Tattler - Orion
Sawbar - Draco


Serpens
-------
Boosted basic stats.
Significantly boosted elemental effects.
Projectiles slither through the air; Boosted projectile speed.


Gemini
------
Functionally similar to the Slagga (still Slag only)
Two projectiles instead of three, damage boosted to compensate.
AIming down sight will burst-fire two rounds.


The Perseids
------------
Launches a swarm of homing spiker rounds that detonate on impact.


Aries
------
Notably Boosted stats (still Corrosive only though)
Heavily boosted elemental effects.
Successful hits will produce health orbs that home in on the user.


Cassiopeia
----------
Functionally similar to the Badaboom.
Five projectiles instead of six, damage boosted to compensate.

Orion
------
Fires one round that divides into three upon impact with a surface.
Splash damage (including Explosive splash for non-elemental versions)


Draco
-----
Dual-element weapon with large splash radius. Incendiary and Explosive.
Boosted damage and fire rate.
